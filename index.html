PROFILE

I’m currently a postdoctoral Research Fellow at Seoul National Univerisity and affiliated with Bio & Health Informatics Lab. I received a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science & Engineering in 2019. My research area focuses on developing machine learning algorithms and tools for analyzing high throughput next generation sequencing data from various organisms, such as, rice, Arabidopsis, mouse and human.
